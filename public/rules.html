How to play the game.
Gameplay:

All you need to do to play the game is have an account on the site (You can get one here if you don't have one) and a good brain for math! Just navigate to https://mathattackerz.ga/game.html to get started. Choose your mode (Addition or Multiplication), and then answer the questions. The questions will be read aloud to you, and all you need to do is type in the answer. If it is correct you will recieve a certian amount of points, and if it is wrong you will lose 2 points.

Scoring:

MathAttackerz uses complex algorithms to give you your score. Your score for each question is the sum of a random number between 0-10 and the average of the two numbers in the question, times your multiplier. Your multiplier is given by a complex algorithm your level.

SCORE: = (Random number from 0 to 10 + The average of the two numbers in your question) x Your Multiplier

Leveling up

In short terms, levels make your multiplier go up, and that's really it! There are complex algorithms that decide when you level up, but here is a approximate list of what score you need to have to level up:

  • 0 - Level 1
  • 500 - Level 2
  • 700 - Level 3
  • 2100 - Level 4
  • 10000 - Level 5

The multipliers for levels can get really really high! For example, on Level 21, the multiplier is over 2,000,000 points, which gives an average of 18,000,000 points a round!
